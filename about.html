About Us

Know About Our Camp

HourCamp is a recreational camping site that promises quality time with fun adventures, without the stresses of technology. It is established in Tanay, Rizal which is an eco-friendly campsite where we unplug ourselves from technology and plug ourselves back into nature and its blessings.

Taking a step back and unplugging an HourCamp takes you back to unwind and embrace nature with our engaging activities. Experience nature, unwind, and relax as we disconnect from the hustle and bustle of the digital world and remove the problems of the real world, and reconnect with the tranquility of unadulterated mother nature.

HOURCAMP

Vision & Mission

To establish HourCamp as a well-known recreational site that encourages everyone to reunite with one’s family, friends, and nature. Providing an eco-friendly camping site for people who are seeking to experience recreational activities away from technology.

VISION

HourCamp mission is to provide unforgettable experiences through leisure and recreational activities away from technology that will meet the guests quality expectations and make them come back for more. The business intends to properly sustain, pr otect and improve the natural resources within the place.

MISSION

HourCamp Recreational Site intends to offer various entertaining recreational activities and showcase a mesmerizing scenic view to encourage people to appreciate mother nature and be one with it. Hourcamp aims to be the first ever recreational site in the Philippines to implement a “technology free” campsite whereas guests will be required to surrender their phones and fully engage in the recreational activities the camp has to offer.

OBJECTIVES

To make people reconnect with their loved ones without any cellphones while at the camp. To hire employees that are able to adapt to any situation. To make a profit after achieving a return on investment in a timely manner.

OBJECTIVES

To have good feedback on online sites to have a pleasing image. To exceed clients' expectations in order for them to be motivated to return.

OBJECTIVES

HourCamp is a partnership type of organization, which allows many owners to coordinate with each other in order to provide the highest quality of activities to visitors.
