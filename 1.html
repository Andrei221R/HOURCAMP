Nature Sketch & Photography

NATURE SKETCH: A full set of art supplies will be provided to each visitor so they can express themselves by means of nature. A blank canvas, an easel stand, along with art supplies (such as paint or charcoal). Visitors are still welcome to borrow the aforementioned goods from the camp, but they may also bring their own supplies and paint to use anywhere they choose inside the camp.

NATURE PHOTOGRPAHY: Each visitor will get 45 minutes to photograph the magnificent beauty of the surroundings. Although we will have photographers accompany them on their time being, soft copies will be issued to them before they leave the camp.
