HourCamp is Located in Tanay Rizal

The products and services that HourCamp offers are originally made to escape and create unforgettable leisure experiences away from stress and technology. The guests have a lot of choices to choose whether they would like to experience adventurous, relaxing, exploratory. The business offers various activities under the acronym of F.R.E.E; Fun Camping Activities, Relaxing Activities, Exploratory Camping Activities and Evening Escapade Activities.

As our lives are more and more entangled with gadgets and the internet, we sometimes need to take a break and return to our roots and remember to detoxify ourselves from the stresses of digitization.

Frequently Asked Question

Any Question?

  • Where Are We Located?

    We are situated in Rizal's Mt. Ayngat Tanay. Astonishing mountain of Mt. Ayngat offers breathtaking views of the Sierra Madre Mountain Range. Standing more than 600 meters above sea level, it is well known for its hiking trails.

  • What Makes Us Unique?

    HourCamp Recreational Site intends to offer various entertaining recreational activities and showcase a mesmerizing scenic view to encourage people to appreciate mother nature and be one with it. Hourcamp aims to be the first ever recreational site in the Philippines to implement a “technology free” campsite whereas guests will be required to surrender their phones and fully engage in the recreational activities the camp has to offer.

  • Do We Accept Children or Is There Any Age Limit?

    We are not imposing an age restriction on who we accept. This is a family-oriented camping site that welcomes everyone. Even though some activities are limited to specific age groups, HourCamp's beauty is still accessible to all.

  • Where Can we Contact you?

    in the website here you can find the social media pages of the camp, in Facebook and Instagram there you can contact us for more further questions and you can contact us through email and our cellphone number for booking inquries and other concerns. Thank you

  • Do You Accept Walk-in?

    As for HOURCAMP, we dont accept walk-in to ensure that we can accomodate the needs and wants of the customers during their stay. in addition we promote booking booking style to avoid overcrowding and related incidents.
